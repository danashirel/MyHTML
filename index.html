QA and Automation - Embarking on a new journey


With all my love for the baking domain, I decided to learn the QA and Automation world because I felt I needed a change.
I wanted to learn something new, to challenge myself, and to do something that was out of my comfort zone.
In our family, my husband is the Tech guy (well, me too, but he's the pro between us).
He introduced me to QA and Automation. It intrigued me and after a short conversation with Gal Matalon (mentor and leading lecturer at the "Automation College"), I decided that it was the new path I was going to take.
